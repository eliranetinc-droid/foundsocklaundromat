If your last laundromat trip was a decade ago, the change-machine and roll-of-quarters routine is gone at most modern laundromats — including ours. **Card readers do everything**, and you have more payment options than you probably expect.

Here's exactly how it works at The Found Sock if you've never been (or it's been a while).

## The simple version

Every washer and dryer has a small touch screen on the front called a **FasCard reader**. To pay, you do one of three things:

1. **Tap a credit or debit card** (Visa, MasterCard, Discover, Amex)
2. **Tap your phone with Apple Pay or Google Pay**
3. **Tap a loyalty card** (you can pre-load it with cash at the kiosk)

The machine starts. That's it.

## What if I only have cash?

This is the question we get most. **Yes — you can use cash.** Just not directly at the machines.

Here's how:

1. Walk to the **touchscreen kiosk** at the front of the store.
2. Tap "**Buy a new card**" (one-time $1.50 fee, never expires).
3. Insert cash to load it. Any amount works, $1 minimum, no maximum.
4. Take the card to any washer or dryer. Tap to pay.

Bonus: that loyalty card earns you **10% cashback on every wash** automatically. So your $20 cash is really worth $22 over time.

## Step by step for a first-time visit

### 1. Bring detergent
Or buy a single-load pack at the kiosk for a couple of dollars. Powder, liquid, and pods all available.

### 2. Pick a washer by load size
- **20 lb** — about 1–2 typical loads ($6.00)
- **30 lb** — about 3 loads or a small comforter ($6.25)
- **35 lb** — bigger comforters or 4 loads ($6.50)
- **45 lb** — king-size comforter or 4+ loads ($7.75)

When in doubt, size up. A washer that's 80% full cleans better than one stuffed full.

### 3. Load clothes, add detergent, close the door

### 4. Tap to pay
Tap a card / phone / loyalty card on the FasCard reader. Pick your wash temperature on the washer's own buttons (cold/warm/hot). Press start.

### 5. Wait or come back later
Most washes are 25–35 minutes. We have free Wi-Fi, comfortable seating, and a TV — or you can leave and come back. **Better yet, download the [FasCard app](/app)** — it'll send you a push notification the moment your cycle finishes.

### 6. Move clothes to a dryer
Dryers are on the opposite wall. Pay the same way ($0.50 buys 4 minutes — most loads need 24–36 minutes). The app can start a dryer too — just scan the QR code on the reader.

### 7. Fold at the steel tables
We have big stainless steel folding stations. You can use them as long as you like.

## Why card-only is actually better than coins

A few reasons we made the switch:

- **No making change.** No "I only have a $20 and the change machine is broken."
- **No machines to break.** Card readers fail less often than coin slots.
- **You can track what you spent.** It's all in your card statement or the FasCard app.
- **Refunds are easier.** If a machine eats your money, we can refund the card directly.

## Common questions

**Q: What if I forget my card and my phone is dead?**
A: We have a kiosk that gives you a new loyalty card for $1.50 — you can load it with any amount of cash on the spot. Problem solved.

**Q: Will the machine charge me even if I don't push start?**
A: No. The reader pre-authorizes the charge but won't actually bill until the cycle starts. If you change your mind, the hold drops off in a few days.

**Q: Can I use a foreign credit card?**
A: Yes, as long as it's a chip card with contactless tap. We've had visitors from all over.
