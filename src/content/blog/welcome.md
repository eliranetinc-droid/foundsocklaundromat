If you're reading this on a phone — and most of you are — you probably noticed this page loaded almost instantly. That wasn't an accident.

We rebuilt the entire site from scratch this month. The homepage now loads in well under a second on a regular 4G phone and scores in the top range on Google's PageSpeed test. The whole page weighs about as much as a single social media photo.

Why does that matter? Two reasons. **First**, you don't waste data when you're checking our hours from the bus. **Second**, Google generally rewards fast, well-built sites, which means more Brighton, Allston, and Brookline neighbors should find us when they're searching for a laundromat nearby.

A few other things we changed:
- The site now shows **whether we're open right now**, automatically.
- Every detail about our [pricing](/pricing/), the [loyalty card](/loyalty/), and the [FasCard app](/app/) is one tap away.
- A new [blog](/blog/) with practical guides — how to wash a comforter, what to bring on your first visit, how to remove tough stains, and more.

That's it. Same friendly neighborhood spot, just on a much better website.

— Found Sock Laundromat
