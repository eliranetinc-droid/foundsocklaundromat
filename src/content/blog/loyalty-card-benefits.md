If you do laundry once a week at The Found Sock, the **loyalty card pays for itself the second time you wash**. We sell the card at the kiosk for **$1.50 once** — and the 10% cashback on every wash means you've made that money back after spending about $15 with us.

Most regulars use the card without thinking about it. But there are a few less-obvious advantages worth knowing.

## What the loyalty card actually does

Every time you tap the card to pay for a washer or dryer, **10% of what you paid is credited back to the card balance**. That balance never expires. So your "$6 wash" is really $5.40 once you account for the cashback that lands back on the card.

Three small things that surprise people:

1. **Cashback applies to dryers too.** Not just washers. A long dry cycle stacks up cashback fast.
2. **The card never expires** — and neither does the balance you load on it. If you take a month off, your money is still there.
3. **You can load it with cash.** That's a big one. We don't take cash at the machines, but the touchscreen kiosk takes cash and converts it to card balance instantly.

## Loading the card from your phone

If you don't want to visit the kiosk to add money, you don't have to. Download the [free FasCard app](/app/) and you can:

- Reload your card balance from anywhere with a credit card
- Check your balance any time
- See your transaction history

For new customers, the typical first visit is: **buy the card at the kiosk → load $20 cash → use the app from then on**.

## Why we offer cashback (and why we don't run other promotions)

A lot of laundromats run "Tuesday is cheaper" or "free dry with paid wash" type promotions. We don't. We picked one simple thing instead: a flat **10% off everything, every time, for everyone with a card**. No coupons, no app codes, no time windows.

That's it. The math is the same whether you wash on Tuesday at 9 AM or Saturday at 6 PM.

## When it adds up

Here's what cashback looks like over a year for a typical customer doing one large load (45 lb washer + 24 minutes of drying) per week:

- Per visit: ~$10.50
- Per year: ~$546
- Cashback at 10%: **~$55 back on your card**

That's about 10 free washes per year, on top of the convenience.

## Pick one up next time you're in

The card is at the kiosk in the front of the store. **Next visit**: tap "Buy a new card" on the touchscreen, pay $1.50 + load any amount you want, and you're set forever.
