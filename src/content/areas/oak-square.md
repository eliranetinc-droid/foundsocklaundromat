## Laundromat near Oak Square

Oak Square neighbors find us about **5 minutes by car** down Washington Street, or an easy 8-minute walk along the same road. **Free street parking right out front** means you can run in, start a load, and run errands — or wait with Wi-Fi and a TV.

If you live in Oak Square or near Faneuil, we're the closest fully card-operated laundromat with the kind of big washers a family actually needs (no chaining four small loads through a building washer).

### What makes us a fit for Oak Square families

- **Big-load washers.** Our 45 lb Electrolux machines handle a king-size comforter or three weeks of family laundry in one cycle.
- **Fast.** Wash + dry in under 90 minutes for a typical mixed load. Even faster if you run two washers in parallel.
- **Cash welcome.** Load any amount onto a [loyalty card](/loyalty/) at our touchscreen kiosk and tap to pay at any machine. Earns 10% cashback every wash.
- **Open every day, 6 AM to 11 PM** — fits around school pickup, weekend errands, kids' sports, anything.
- **Family-friendly.** Strollers welcome, kids welcome, free Wi-Fi for when you want them quietly watching something for 30 minutes.

### Getting here from Oak Square

- **Driving:** Head east on Washington Street toward Brighton Center. We're at 76 Washington — free 2-hour street parking out front and along the block.
- **Walking:** ~8 minutes south down Washington Street. Sidewalk the whole way.
- **By bus:** The **57 bus** stops nearby and connects Oak Square to Brighton and beyond.
- **Cycling:** ~4 minutes flat along Washington Street.

### Tips for Oak Square regulars

- Buy a loyalty card on your first visit. $1.50 once, never expires, earns 10% cashback every time you tap. Most regulars pay for the card with their second visit.
- For comforters and oversized blankets, the 45 lb washer is the right call — see our [comforter guide](/blog/how-to-wash-a-comforter/) for sizing.
- **Quiet times:** weekday mornings before 10 AM, Tuesday and Wednesday afternoons. Avoid Sunday 11 AM–4 PM (the busiest window).
- For school-uniform-heavy laundry, our [too-much-detergent post](/blog/too-much-detergent/) explains how to dial in the right amount so uniforms don't come out stiff.

### Good to know

- We're self-service only — no wash-and-fold or drop-off. You wash, you fold, you go.
- Detergent: bring your own or buy a single-load pack at the kiosk.
- Machine issues or refunds: use the [report-an-issue form](/report-issue/) — it lands as a ticket and we reply within a few hours during open hours.
