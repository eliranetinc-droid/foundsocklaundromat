## Looking for a laundromat in Allston?

The Found Sock is the closest fully card-operated laundromat to Allston. We're about a **6-minute walk** from Allston center, right over the Brighton line at 76 Washington Street.

### Why Allston residents come to us
- **No quarters needed.** Pay with any credit card, Apple Pay, Google Pay, or a loyalty card.
- **Big washers.** 30 lb, 35 lb, and 45 lb machines for comforters and bulky loads.
- **Start machines from your phone.** Download the FasCard app, see what's open, scan and start.
- **Late hours.** Open until 11 PM every day — handy for the BU/BC schedule.
- **Free Wi-Fi & seating.** Get work done while your laundry runs.

### Getting here from Allston
- **Walking from Harvard Ave + Brighton Ave:** Walk south on Harvard Ave for about 6 minutes. Right on Washington St.
- **Driving:** Free street parking right out front.
- **By bus:** 64 and 86 stop within a few blocks.

### Tips for Allston students
- A 45 lb washer handles 2–3 weeks of regular laundry in one cycle — much cheaper per pound than weekly small loads.
- Tuesday and Wednesday mornings are our quietest times. Avoid Sunday 11 AM – 4 PM (peak hours).
- Tennis balls in the dryer help with comforters and pillows.
