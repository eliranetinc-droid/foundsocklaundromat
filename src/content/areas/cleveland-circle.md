## A laundromat for Cleveland Circle

If you're in Cleveland Circle and need a real laundromat — not the tiny one in the corner of your building — we're a **7-minute drive** down Washington Street. Or hop on the 57 bus.

### Cleveland Circle students and residents come to us for
- **More washers, less waiting.** Our 30, 35, and 45 lb machines mean you don't have to use 4 small ones.
- **Phone-controlled machines.** The FasCard app lets you start a wash from your apartment and walk over when it's almost done.
- **No coins.** Pay by tap with any card or Apple Pay.
- **Free Wi-Fi & TV.** A pleasant place to wait, not a dingy basement.

### Getting here from Cleveland Circle
- **Driving:** Take Chestnut Hill Ave to Beacon, right on Washington — 7 min.
- **Bus:** The 57 (Watertown Yard via Brighton) drops you a block away.
- **Walking:** ~12 minutes through Brighton.

### Pro tip
- Sundays and weekday evenings are busiest. Saturday mornings before 10 AM are usually quiet.
