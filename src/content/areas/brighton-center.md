## A laundromat right in your neighborhood

If you live in Brighton Center, you've probably walked past us on Washington Street. We're **4 minutes from Brighton Center** by foot — head south past Washington Square and you're here. The closest fully card-operated laundromat to anyone living near St. Elizabeth's, Brighton Branch Library, or the Comm Ave / Washington Street intersection.

### What Brighton Center neighbors love

- **Walk over, start a load, walk home.** Use the [FasCard app](/app/) to get notified when it's done — or wait with free Wi-Fi and a TV.
- **Cash-friendly via our kiosk.** Don't want to use your credit card? Load a [loyalty card](/loyalty/) with cash at the touchscreen kiosk, then tap at any machine. Earns 10% cashback every wash forever.
- **Family-size washers.** 30, 35, and 45 lb machines so you can wash everything in one shot instead of three smaller cycles.
- **Always open.** 6 AM to 11 PM every day, including holidays.
- **Free street parking.** Right out front on Washington Street (2-hour limit, plenty of turnover during the day).

### Getting here from Brighton Center

- **Walking:** South on Washington Street, about 4 minutes from Washington Square. Sidewalk the whole way.
- **Driving:** Free street parking right out front (2-hour limit).
- **By bus:** Multiple lines stop nearby — the 57, 64, and 65 are all options depending on where you're coming from.
- **By bike:** ~3 minutes along Washington Street.

### Tips for Brighton Center regulars

- **Buy a loyalty card on your first visit** — it pays for itself within 2 visits ($1.50 once, earns 10% cashback every wash, never expires).
- **Mid-week mornings (Tuesday–Wednesday, before 11 AM) are the calmest** times. Avoid Sunday 11 AM–4 PM (full weekly schedule [here](/blog/best-time-to-visit/)).
- For seasonal comforter washes, the **45 lb washer is the right call** — see our [comforter guide](/blog/how-to-wash-a-comforter/).
- If you're new to a laundromat, the [first-visit checklist](/blog/first-laundromat-visit-checklist/) covers everything you need.

### Good to know

- **Self-service only.** We don't offer wash-and-fold or drop-off — every customer does their own. The trade-off is no waiting on someone else and lower pricing.
- **Detergent:** bring your own or buy a single-load pack at the kiosk for a couple of dollars.
- **For machine issues or refunds**, use the [report-an-issue form](/report-issue/) — it lands as a ticket and we respond within a few hours during open hours.
